Assignments

The following cases will test your detective skills. Solve each mystery and submit a detailed report of your methods and conclusions by the due date. All assignments are to be submitted via GitHub Classroom (as private repositories).

  • Case 1: The Missing Manuscript – Due Sept 30. In this assignment, investigate the disappearance of an invaluable manuscript from the university archives. Apply techniques from Weeks 1–4 and provide your conclusions.
  • Case 2: The Whispering Walls – Due Oct 28. Tackle a mystery involving strange occurrences at a city museum. Analyze provided witness statements and security logs to identify the culprit.
  • Final Case: The Ultimate Deduction – Due Dec 10. A comprehensive case tying together all course concepts. Present your findings to the class in the final session.

Detailed instructions and data for each case will be handed out and posted here as the course progresses. Stay sharp and remember: "Once you eliminate the impossible, whatever remains, no matter how improbable, must be the truth."
