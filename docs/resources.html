Resources

Here are some recommended materials to assist you on your journey to becoming a master detective:

Books & Stories

  • The Complete Sherlock Holmes by A. Conan Doyle – Collection of Holmes stories (primary inspiration for case scenarios).
  • Forensic Science for Beginners by J. Doe – An introduction to modern forensic techniques and how they relate to classic detective work.
  • The Art of Deduction by M. Adrian – A contemporary analysis of Holmes's methods applied to real-life problem solving.

Online Resources

  • 221B Baker Street Archives – An online repository of Sherlock Holmes references and historical context for the stories.
  • Forensic Fundamentals – Interactive tutorials on fingerprinting, chemical analysis, and other forensic methods.
  • Victorian London Map – Useful for visualizing the setting of classic Holmes tales (for fun and reference).

Tools

  • Magnifying glass (provided in class kit).
  • Fingerprint dusting kit (for lab exercises).
  • Secure notebook – keep detailed notes like Dr. Watson.
