About This Course

"Education never ends, Watson. It is a series of lessons with the greatest for the last." – Sherlock Holmes

This course is a creative homage to the world of Sherlock Holmes. It was designed to make learning interactive and fun by blending classic detective fiction with modern educational practice. Students are challenged to think like Holmes and document their process like Watson.

Instructors & Team

  • Sherlock Holmes – (Instructor) Renowned consulting detective turned guest lecturer, sharing his expertise in deduction.
  • Dr. John H. Watson – (Teaching Assistant) Holmes's loyal friend and chronicler, assisting students and providing feedback on case reports.
  • Professor Moriarty – (Guest Speaker) Lectures on criminal psychology and the art of misdirection (strictly under supervision!).

Contact

For any course-related inquiries, please contact us at holmes@221b.edu or post on the course forum. Office hours are held at 221B Baker Street (and via video call for remote participants) every Wednesday at 7 PM.
